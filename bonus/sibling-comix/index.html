“Sibling comics, with Kade and Rudy!” A series of four-panel strips.

In the teaser, Rudy torments his sibling: “Hey, Kade. Hey, Kade. Hey Kade. Hey Kade. Hey Kade. Hey Kade.” etc. etc.

Kade finally snaps: “What.”

Rudy holds out a folded piece of paper and declares proudly: “New comic!”

Kade, mildly exasperated, just stares.
