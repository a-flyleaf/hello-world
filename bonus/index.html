Val is a light-skinned redhead with a long, swooping ponytail. Her outfit is simple and three-toned: black shrug and leggings, white dress, and red boots. Usually drawn with a stylized eyelash.
Kade is a brown-skinned brunette with short, messy hair. Blue long-sleeved shirt with two lighter stripes, black jeans, blue-and-white velcro sneakers, and often wears a faded beanie & waist-tied sweater. Though not depicted here, wears a heart choker in non-flashback scenes unless otherwise noted. Usually has dark eyebags.
Cyrus is a dark-skinned blonde with straight, medium-length hair that, in game scenes, is shown covering their right eye with an “emo bang.” They wear a gray vest over an off-white button down, dark pants tucked into clunky black boots, and can often be seen readjusting or grabbing a three-striped tie.
Rudy is a brown-skinned brunette with zigzag hair often tied back in a small ponytail. Sports a red hat and ripped shorts, black tank top with four dots in the pattern of a separated clover or right-hand video game buttons, black-and-white flip-flops and belt, and green-striped armwarmers with all fingers but the middle cut out.
All four have stylized oval pupils, no visible irises, and a grayscale inner mouth.
